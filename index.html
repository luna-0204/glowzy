💫 How To Use

  1. Take a small spoon of powder into your palm 🥄
  2. Add a few drops of liquid of your choice 💧
  3. Mix into a paste, massage gently
  4. Rinse off with water, pat dry 🧫
  5. Use daily or a few times a week
